Allergy


Q: What are common allergens, and how can they be avoided?

A: Common allergens include pollen, dust mites, pet dander, mold, certain foods, insect stings, and specific medications. Avoidance strategies may include using allergen-proof covers, regular cleaning, avoiding known food allergens, and consulting with an allergist for personalized guidance.

Q: How can I manage seasonal allergies?

A: Seasonal allergies can be managed through the use of antihistamines, nasal sprays, or allergy shots, along with avoiding outdoor activities during peak pollen times, keeping windows closed, and using air purifiers.

Q: What should I do if I suspect a food allergy?

A: If you suspect a food allergy, it is essential to consult with a healthcare provider or allergist. They can conduct tests to identify the specific allergen and provide guidance on avoiding the allergenic food and managing reactions.
